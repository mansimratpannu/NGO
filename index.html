Our Mission

Protect. Preserve. Empower.

To protect and preserve the environment while empowering communities to live healthier, safer, and more sustainable lives. We believe that a healthy environment is the very foundation of a healthy society.

Our Vision

Harmony Between Nature & Humanity

A world where nature and humanity coexist in harmony, ensuring a greener planet and a better future for generations to come — where every action today shapes the tomorrow we deserve.

Our Work

What We Do

🌱

Environmental Awareness & Education

Conducting seminars, workshops, and campaigns to spread awareness about environmental protection, sustainable practices, and the importance of biodiversity.

🌳

Tree Plantation & Cleanliness Drives

Organising large-scale tree plantation initiatives and community cleanliness drives to restore green cover and keep public spaces healthy and beautiful.

🤝

Community Welfare & Development

Supporting community welfare programmes that uplift society, address local environmental challenges, and create pathways for sustainable development.

♻️

Sustainable Practices Advocacy

Advocating for eco-friendly living and sustainable practices across households, schools, and institutions to reduce environmental footprint.

💧

Water Conservation & Pollution Control

Working actively towards water conservation, prevention of water pollution, and educating communities on responsible resource management.

On the Ground

Recent Events

Awareness Seminar · Gurdaspur

Environmental Awareness Seminar — Village Bhukra

An environmental awareness seminar was successfully organised at Government Primary School, Village Bhukra, District Gurdaspur. The seminar educated students and the local community about the importance of environmental protection, tree plantation, cleanliness, and adopting sustainable practices for a healthier and greener future.

Awareness Program · Bal Bhawan

Environmental Awareness Programme — Bal Bhawan, Gurdaspur

A meaningful environmental awareness programme was conducted at Bal Bhawan, District Gurdaspur. The initiative focused on encouraging young minds and the local community to understand the importance of protecting the environment, conserving natural resources, and adopting eco-friendly habits to build a sustainable and healthier future.
